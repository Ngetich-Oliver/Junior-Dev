About Us

The independence electoral commission is an indepent constitutional commission established by the consitution of our republic in order to oversee two various responsibilities,that is :

  1. Conducting free and fair elections
  2. Overseeing Boundary Delimation across the country
The Commission also when called upon can conduct elections within the organisations in the republic such as Universities,Political Parties and Law Society of Kenya.We are ,located in Annivesary Towers and are always open 5 days a week within the working times. Our aim is to be free and fair to all the people we serve

Services

Elecction management

Persuant to the artile 120(2) of the constitution of Kenya, the IEBC shall after every five years register candidates for elcective seats.The commission must ensure that the candidates have fully adhered to the requirements of the constitution but not limited to chapter 4.The commission shall then print ballot papers and on the election day ensure they are availed to all polling stations across the country.After the polls are closed the votes will be tallied and send to returning officer office of each county.All the results will be released however the Presidential once will be uploaded to the IEBC portal for National Tallying and after not more than 7 days the Chairperson must announce the results of the Presidential election.

Border Delimation

The IEBC shall after 10 years review the Constituency and subcounty boundaries based on the population of the region in order to ensure equitable distribution of resources.The new boundaries are to be gazzetted in order for the appropriate actions to be taken
